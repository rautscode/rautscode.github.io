Sebastian Rautenberg
I always thought I love computers. It turns out that I am obsessed with eliminating complex business problems using automation and DevOps models in public cloud environments, especially Azure. I remember when I first heard about Public Cloud, I thought “Hold on, vSphere client in my browser, but without me having to manage the physical fabric, this is the future”. Just like the rest of the world - I was right. I completely redirected my attention to learning technologies and practices such as Azure, PowerShell, Configuration Management Tools, Software Development, DevOps, CICD Pipelines, SDLC processes, primarily everything that would enable businesses to transform the world of hosting and operations into automated environments that allow businesses to focus on its primary goals. I specialize in: - Planning, designing and implementing fully automated enterprise-level application hosting environments in Public Cloud - Developing scalable CICD and SDLC process for software development teams - Documenting and designing business and operations processes around the hosting environments - Securing and conducting Technical Risk Assessments for Public Cloud environments and web applications - Designing and building modern data warehouse systems for big data ETL - Helping application teams develop application hosting infrastructures and adopting CICD processes - Managing projects and project delivery
